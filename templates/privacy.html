Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the Spendly service — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and support filtering and search. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is accessible only to you through your account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This results in a request to Google's servers when you load the app. Beyond this, we do not share your data with any third-party service, analytics provider, or advertiser.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out at support@spendly.app.
